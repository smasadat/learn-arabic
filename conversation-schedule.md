## 🔁 The Repeat Formula (Spaced Repetition — 4 Steps)

Every topic follows this 4-step cycle after its first introduction:

| Step | Label | When | Duration | Activity |
|------|-------|------|----------|----------|
| 🟢 | **Learn** | Day 1 of topic | 15–20 min | Full dialogue script — read aloud, then speak from memory |
| 🔵 | **Review** | Day 1 + **3 days** | 5–7 min | Recall 5 key sentences without notes |
| 🟡 | **Deepen** | Day 1 + **7 days** | 10 min | Free short conversation using topic context |
| 🔴 | **Master** | Day 1 + **14 days** | 7 min | Answer 3 practice questions spontaneously |

> **Golden Rule**: When a topic is labelled 🔵/🟡/🔴, do NOT re-read the script — speak freely. The effort of recall is where real fluency is built.

## ✅ Tips for Success

1. **Daughter sessions**: Keep them natural and fun — in the kitchen, at the park, during dinner. Let the topic match what you are actually doing.
2. **Ustaaj sessions**: Ask for correction every time. Tell him which topic it is so he can focus on the right vocabulary.
3. **Don't skip review steps**: 🔵 Review on Day+3 is the most important step. Without it, new words disappear within a week.
4. **Use topic vocabulary daily**: If it's Kitchen week, try to name everything you touch in the kitchen in Arabic throughout the day.
5. **Track your progress**: Tick off each day in this schedule as you go. Seeing progress is motivating.
6. **Missing a day**: If you miss a day, don't skip — just shift the schedule forward by one day. Consistency matters more than speed.
